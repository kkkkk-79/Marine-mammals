Dolphins

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and complex social structures. They belong to the cetacean family and are closely related to whales and porpoises. Dolphins are found in oceans and seas worldwide, with some species also living in freshwater habitats.

Here is some key information about dolphins:

Feature Details
Scientific Name Delphinidae
Average Lifespan 20-50 years
Diet Fish, squid, and other marine creatures
Habitat Oceans, seas, and some rivers
Notable Trait Highly intelligent and capable of communication
