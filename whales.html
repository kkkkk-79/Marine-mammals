Whales

Whales are the largest marine mammals and are part of the cetacean family. They are found in oceans worldwide and are known for their intelligence, social behavior, and complex vocalizations, including songs.

Here is some key information about whales:

Feature Details
Scientific Name Cetacea
Average Lifespan 30-90 years
Diet Krill, fish, and plankton
Habitat Oceans worldwide
Notable Trait Large size and unique vocalization patterns
